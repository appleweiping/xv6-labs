== xv6 Lab: net (Networking / e1000 driver) — grade-lab-net ==
Host: WSL2 Ubuntu, QEMU riscv64 TCG emulation.
Implementation: kernel/e1000.c e1000_transmit() and e1000_recv() written
from the MIT skeleton stubs. TX: check the tail descriptor's DD bit for ring
overflow, free the previous mbuf in the slot, program addr/length/cmd
(RS|EOP), advance TDT with a memory barrier. RX: drain every descriptor whose
DD bit is set, hand each mbuf to net_rx(), refill with a fresh mbuf, advance
RDT. e1000_lock guards the TX ring.
